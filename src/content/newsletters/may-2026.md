## Georgia Event Showcases New Multimodal Trip Planning Tool

![GDOT ribbon cutting](https://content.govdelivery.com/attachments/fancy_images/USDOTFHWA/2026/05/13664130/6886052/source-gwinnett-county-usdot-ribbon-cutting_crop.jpg)

*Source: Gwinnett County*

Community leaders, local transportation officials, and representatives of the U.S. Department of Transportation gathered in Gwinnett County, Georgia, to announce the launch of a mobile app that helps people plan trips using multiple transportation modes, including walking and transit.

The open-source Georgia Mobility Access Map (G-MAP) application improves safety and mobility by supporting door-to-door trip planning and more accessible route information.

> "Today's launch of G-MAP in Gwinnett County demonstrates how federal innovation programs like ITS4US can deliver real-world solutions that improve mobility and independence for everyone."

[Visit the ITS4US GDOT Deployment Webpage](https://www.its.dot.gov/research-areas/ITS4US/deployments/georgia/)
