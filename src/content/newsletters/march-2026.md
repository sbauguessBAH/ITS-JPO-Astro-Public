## Announcements

### Regular Engagement with Growing Accelerating V2X Cohort

![V2X Cohort Map](https://content.govdelivery.com/attachments/fancy_images/USDOTFHWA/2026/02/13221744/6730834/v2x-cohort-map3-1_crop.png)

*Source: USDOT*

The Accelerating Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) Cohort includes 74 public agencies that meet monthly to share experiences, challenges, and best practices for planning and deploying V2X technologies. The January meeting featured presentations from Security Credential Management System (SCMS) providers on current and future capabilities, deployment experience, and lessons learned.

Public agencies interested in participating in the cohort should contact [john.schneeberger@dot.gov](mailto:john.schneeberger@dot.gov).

[Learn More](https://www.its.dot.gov/resources/pcb/technical-assistance/v2x-cohort/)

### Intersection Safety Challenge Validation Data Released

![Intersection Safety Challenge validation](https://content.govdelivery.com/attachments/fancy_images/USDOTFHWA/2026/02/13189603/isc-validation_original.png)

*Source: USDOT*

On February 17, 2026, ITS JPO released additional data from the Intersection Safety Challenge. The 41 Stage 1B validation data runs (approximately 30 GB), labeled with associated ground truth, are now available on ITS DataHub to support researchers and practitioners improving AI algorithms.

[Download Data](https://data.transportation.gov/Roadways-and-Bridges/Intersection-Safety-Challenge-Stage-1B-Validation-/j58q-ymi4/about_data)

### The SCRC Website is Live!

![SCRC website](https://content.govdelivery.com/attachments/fancy_images/USDOTFHWA/2026/02/13176982/6730778/scrc-main-page_crop.png)

*Source: USDOT*

The Smart Community Resource Center (SCRC) website is now available to the public. Designed for states, tribal governments, and local communities, SCRC is an online repository with resources for developing ITS and smart community transportation programs, including materials on safety, V2X, automation, transit innovation, cybersecurity, systems engineering, and performance measurement.

[Visit Website](https://www.its.dot.gov/scrc/#/)

### ITSPAC Seeking Member Nominations by March 16

The U.S. Department of Transportation is seeking 25 members for the Intelligent Transportation Systems Program Advisory Committee (ITSPAC) to provide expert advice on ITS program needs and technologies. The committee was re-established in December 2025 and will include balanced representation from metropolitan and rural interests across public agencies, the private sector, and academia.

**Nominations were due by March 16, 2026.**

[View Full Notice](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/02/13/2026-02930/intelligent-transportation-systems-program-advisory-committee-agency-office-of-the-secretary-of)
