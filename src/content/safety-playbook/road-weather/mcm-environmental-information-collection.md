The MCM Environmental Information Collection system is a comprehensive solution for monitoring and assessing environmental conditions on roadways. It collects real-time data from a network of environmental sensors deployed along roads and mounted on maintenance vehicles.

Key parameters include road surface temperature, moisture levels, ice formation, salinity, and overall weather conditions. By aggregating this data, the system provides a detailed and continuously updated picture of roadway conditions, helping transportation agencies maintain safe and efficient travel.

In addition to condition assessment, the system serves as a critical information source for weather service providers and operational personnel, ensuring that decision-makers have access to accurate, timely environmental information.

The system also enables remote monitoring and control of sensors, allowing maintenance teams to detect faults, troubleshoot issues, and manage equipment without being physically present at each sensor location.

By integrating real-time monitoring, automated data aggregation, and remote management capabilities, MCM Environmental Information Collection supports proactive transportation management.
