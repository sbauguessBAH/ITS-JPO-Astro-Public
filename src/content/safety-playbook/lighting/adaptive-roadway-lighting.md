Adaptive roadway lighting uses sensor inputs and control algorithms to dynamically adjust light output to current conditions.

By increasing light when visibility demand is higher and reducing output when demand is low, agencies can improve nighttime safety while lowering energy and maintenance costs.

This approach supports sustainable roadway operations and can improve perceived safety for all road users.
