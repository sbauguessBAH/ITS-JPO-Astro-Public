Nighttime visibility improvements address a major safety risk period by increasing detection distance and conspicuity for road users and roadway features.

Treatments include improved lighting, high-performance retroreflective signs and markings, and visibility-aware maintenance practices.

These measures support safer travel for drivers, pedestrians, and cyclists, especially in complex or high-activity corridors.
