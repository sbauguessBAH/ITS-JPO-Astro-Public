Rectangular Rapid Flashing Beacons are active pedestrian crossing treatments that alert motorists when pedestrians are present.

RRFBs are commonly deployed at uncontrolled crossings and can be activated by pushbutton or passive detection, improving visibility and driver awareness.

They are widely used as a cost-effective countermeasure to improve yielding behavior and reduce pedestrian conflict risk.
