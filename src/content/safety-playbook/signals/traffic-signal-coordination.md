Traffic signal coordination aligns signal timing plans along corridors so vehicles can progress through multiple intersections with fewer stops.

Coordination improves travel time reliability, reduces delay, and can lower fuel use and emissions by reducing stop-and-go behavior.

When maintained with current traffic data, coordinated operations provide a high-impact strategy for urban mobility and safety performance.
