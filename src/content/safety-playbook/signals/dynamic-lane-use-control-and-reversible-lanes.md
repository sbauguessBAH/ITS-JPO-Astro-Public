Dynamic lane use control and reversible lane operations enable agencies to reallocate roadway capacity to match directional demand.

Using lane control signs, surveillance, and traffic management systems, agencies can open, close, or reverse lanes to reduce bottlenecks and improve flow.

This strategy can improve reliability and safety by reducing turbulence and providing clearer lane guidance during high-demand periods and events.
