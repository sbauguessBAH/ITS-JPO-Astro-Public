Yellow change interval timing affects driver decision-making at signalized intersections and is a key safety parameter.

Condition-responsive approaches can better align yellow timing with observed speeds and approach characteristics to reduce dilemma zone risk.

These strategies are typically implemented with engineering review and integrated with broader signal timing and safety programs.
