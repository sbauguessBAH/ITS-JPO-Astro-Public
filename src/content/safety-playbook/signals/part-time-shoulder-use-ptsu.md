Part-time Shoulder Use is an active management strategy that allows shoulder lanes to serve traffic under defined operating conditions.

These operations rely on continuous monitoring, lane control messaging, and incident management protocols to maintain safe use.

PTSU can provide meaningful capacity relief where corridor demand exceeds available lane space during peak periods.
