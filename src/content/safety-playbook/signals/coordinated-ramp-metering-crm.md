Coordinated Ramp Metering manages multiple on-ramp signals as a connected system to balance demand and maintain mainline flow.

By using real-time traffic measurements and adaptive control, CRM can reduce merge turbulence, improve throughput, and limit recurring bottlenecks.

This approach is especially valuable in dense freeway corridors where independent metering is insufficient for network-level performance.
