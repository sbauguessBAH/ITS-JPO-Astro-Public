Pedestrian Hybrid Beacons are signalized crossing treatments typically used where pedestrian demand and vehicle speeds make unsignalized crossing difficult.

When activated, PHBs provide clear stop indications to drivers and protected crossing opportunities for pedestrians with high compliance rates.

They are especially effective at improving safety at multilane or high-speed crossings without requiring a full traffic signal installation.
