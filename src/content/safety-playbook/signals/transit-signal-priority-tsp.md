Transit Signal Priority is used to improve the speed and reliability of transit services by modifying signal timing when buses or other transit vehicles approach intersections.

TSP can reduce red-light delay for transit, improve schedule consistency, and increase person-throughput on key corridors with limited impact to general traffic when properly designed.

By integrating vehicle detection, signal controllers, and traffic management systems, TSP supports safer and more efficient multimodal operations in urban and suburban networks.
