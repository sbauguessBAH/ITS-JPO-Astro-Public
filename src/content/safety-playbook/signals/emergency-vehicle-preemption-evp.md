Emergency Vehicle Preemption enables emergency vehicles to request signal priority as they approach intersections, typically by granting green or holding opposing traffic.

This strategy improves emergency response performance and reduces crash risk at intersections by minimizing conflicts between emergency and general traffic.

Modern EVP implementations integrate connected vehicle technologies and centralized operations support for more consistent and safer preemption control.
