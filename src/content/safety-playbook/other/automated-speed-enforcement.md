Automated speed enforcement systems use sensors and imaging to identify vehicles exceeding posted speed limits.

These systems support consistent enforcement and can improve speed compliance, particularly at high-risk locations.

When integrated with broader safety programs, automated enforcement can reduce speed-related crash frequency and severity.
