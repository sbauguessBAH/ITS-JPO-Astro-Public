Bicycle safety systems use sensing and communication technologies to improve awareness of cyclists and reduce crash risk.

Applications include cyclist detection at intersections, warning systems for turning conflicts, and enhanced visibility treatments for low-light environments.

These systems support safer multimodal operations and can complement infrastructure and policy interventions for active transportation.
