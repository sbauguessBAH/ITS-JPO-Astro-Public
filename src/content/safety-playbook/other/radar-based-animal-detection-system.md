Radar-based animal detection systems monitor roadside zones for wildlife movement and trigger driver warnings when animals approach travel lanes.

These systems reduce wildlife-vehicle crash risk and can improve driver behavior in high-risk corridors, especially at night.

They also provide useful data for habitat and crossing management decisions by transportation and wildlife agencies.
