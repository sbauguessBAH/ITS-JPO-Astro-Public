Wrong-way driving detection systems monitor ramps and directional segments for vehicle movements against intended traffic flow.

When events are detected, systems activate driver-facing warnings and notify operations and enforcement teams with event data.

This strategy is used to reduce severe head-on and high-speed conflict crashes, especially during nighttime and low-visibility conditions.
