Automated Flagger Assistance Devices provide stop-slow control in temporary work zones while allowing operators to remain in safer locations.

AFADs improve consistency of control operations, reduce worker exposure to live traffic, and can support efficient traffic movement through constrained segments.

They are an established treatment for one-lane two-way work zones when deployed and operated according to standards.
