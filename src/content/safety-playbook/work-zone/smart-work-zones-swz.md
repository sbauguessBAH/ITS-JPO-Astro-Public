Smart work zones are technologically enhanced construction and maintenance areas designed to improve safety, efficiency, and traffic management. They leverage tools such as queue warning systems, sensors, connected devices, and real-time communication platforms to actively monitor traffic and worker activity.

These systems provide drivers with timely warnings, recommended speeds, and delay information, while enabling agencies to optimize traffic flow and reduce crash risk. Connected assets such as smart barrels, portable sensors, and message boards support dynamic responses as conditions change.

By combining monitoring, analytics, and automated alerts, smart work zones help reduce crashes and delays, improve project delivery, and create safer environments for workers and travelers.
