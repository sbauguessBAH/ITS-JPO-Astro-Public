Computer vision systems use cameras and machine learning models to detect pedestrians and analyze movement in near real time.

These systems support driver warning, intersection safety analytics, and advanced automation applications in complex urban environments.

When paired with traffic control strategies, they can improve vulnerable road user safety and operational decision-making.
