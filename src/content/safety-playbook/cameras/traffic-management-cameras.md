Traffic management cameras support continuous visual monitoring of roadway and intersection conditions for operations centers.

They improve incident detection, verification, and response, while also providing data for congestion management and traffic signal optimization.

When paired with analytics platforms, camera networks provide valuable performance and safety insights for planning and operations.
