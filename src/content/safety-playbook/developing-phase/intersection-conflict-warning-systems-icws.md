Intersection Conflict Warning Systems monitor vehicle approaches and provide warnings when conflict risk is elevated.

ICWS deployments are commonly used at high-risk intersections to improve driver awareness and reduce right-angle and severe injury crash potential.

These systems are a cost-effective active safety treatment that can complement existing signage and control strategies.
