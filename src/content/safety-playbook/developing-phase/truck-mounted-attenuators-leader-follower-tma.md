Leader-Follower TMA systems protect maintenance crews and emergency responders by providing a mobile attenuator buffer vehicle in high-risk roadway operations.

These systems reduce exposure to struck-by events, improve consistency in mobile work zone protection, and support safer deployment for repetitive maintenance activities.

As automation and cooperative driving capabilities expand, leader-follower TMA operations can further improve worker safety and operational efficiency.
