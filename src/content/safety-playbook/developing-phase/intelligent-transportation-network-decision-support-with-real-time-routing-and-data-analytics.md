Intelligent Transportation Network Decision Support with Real-time Routing and Data Analytics is a comprehensive framework that improves transportation network performance through continuous data collection, real-time analytics, and decision support tools.

The system combines information from IoT sensors, GPS-enabled vehicles, connected infrastructure, crowdsourced reports, and geographic information systems (GIS). These sources are processed by big data and AI-driven analytics to provide transportation operators with actionable insights for traffic management, routing, and incident response.

In day-to-day operations, the framework can identify congestion patterns, evaluate incident effects, and recommend route strategies that reduce delays. Predictive capabilities extend this value by forecasting potential disruptions from weather, planned events, or historical demand patterns so operators can proactively stage resources and communicate advisories.

As mobility ecosystems evolve, this approach can support connected and automated vehicle fleet coordination and improve multimodal operations, including transit reliability and demand-responsive adjustments.

Implementation and communication rely on broad network inputs from sensors, cameras, connected-vehicle feeds, and detectors that describe system conditions in near real time. Those data streams are analyzed in operations platforms where traffic states, incident impacts, weather, and demand are fused to produce routing recommendations and network management actions.
