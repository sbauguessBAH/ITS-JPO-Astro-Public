Variable Speed Limits are an active traffic management strategy that adjusts speed limits in real time using traffic and environmental data.

VSL helps reduce crash risk by encouraging speeds appropriate for current conditions, especially during congestion, inclement weather, incidents, and work zones.

When integrated with broader ITS systems, VSL can improve throughput, reduce stop-and-go waves, and support more stable and safer freeway operations.
