Cellular Vehicle-to-Everything (C-V2X) supports low-latency communication between vehicles and the broader transportation environment.

It enables safety and mobility applications such as intersection movement assistance, hazard alerts, and cooperative driving support through V2V, V2I, V2P, and V2N communication modes.

C-V2X is a foundational technology for connected and automated transportation systems that require timely and reliable data exchange.
