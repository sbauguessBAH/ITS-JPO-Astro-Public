Highway Advisory Radio provides voice-based traveler information through dedicated broadcast channels.

It supports work zone communication, incident advisories, weather messaging, and event-related guidance while allowing drivers to keep eyes on the roadway.

HAR remains a useful supplemental communication channel where targeted and localized messaging is needed.
