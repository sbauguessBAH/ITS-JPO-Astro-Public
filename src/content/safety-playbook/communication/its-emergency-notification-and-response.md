ITS emergency notification and response capabilities support rapid dissemination of critical incident information to the public and responders.

These systems integrate detection, communications, and operational coordination tools to improve emergency response performance and reduce secondary incident risk.

They are essential for maintaining situational awareness and effective multi-agency coordination during high-impact events.
